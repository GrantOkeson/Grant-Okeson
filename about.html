About Me

Student · Eagle Scout · Return Missionary

Hi, I'm Grant Okeson — a student at Brigham Young University studying Pre-Global Supply Chain Management. I'm passionate about business operations, data analysis, and finding smarter ways to solve problems.

I'm also recently engaged and couldn't be more excited about that next chapter of life. Between school, work, and wedding planning, things are busy — but in the best way possible.

I've gained real-world experience working at Lgcy Power, where I developed skills in a fast-paced business environment, as well as at Idaho Falls Parks and Recreation. These experiences have shaped how I think about teamwork, customer service, and getting things done.

On the technical side, I work with Excel, SQL, and Tableau — tools I use to analyze data and build visualizations that tell a clear story. I'm always looking to grow my skill set and take on new challenges.

Outside of school and work, I'm proud to be an Eagle Scout and a returned missionary. Both experiences taught me discipline, leadership, and how to serve others — values I carry into everything I do.

Feel free to reach out or connect with me on LinkedIn.
